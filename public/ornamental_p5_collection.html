The Breathing App

Breath in Practice

An abstract instrument for the breath. There are no counts and no numbers — you choose an intention and follow the form as it opens and settles. You are free to mark the field whenever you like; the machine takes its own time to reflect your marks and balance the composition around them. A first touchpoint for a practice that gathers, over time, into a small library of its own.
